Our Story

This store was based off of a fantasy. This shop is the first Fantasy Medieval to open an online store.

Casper Cloud and Peter Parker met in Harvard University in a Witchcraft class. They noticed each other's passion and skills on potions, spells, and

magic. Once they started a small business from the ground up, they knew they had open to offer in this world.

After years and years of hard work, they launched their first store in 2011, and sold over 2 million products within the first year.

Since then, they opened up 30+ stores in Europe. They launched their online website in 2015.

Meet the Founders!

Co-Founder: Casper Cloud

Casper Cloud has a Master's degree in Witchcraft with emphasis in potions. He started a small business of customized

magic potions and lamps to pay for his tuition. Then his love and passion for making potions grew once he graduated and

grew his business with Parker.

Co-Founder: Peter Parker

Peter Parker has a Bachelor's degree in Medieval History with a minor in Business. He loved to learn about history and was curious about Witchcraft

in college, so he takes a class with Cloud and this is how the Founder's meet. Cloud has a business brains when it came to

start-ups. He saw Cloud's store and wanted to be his business partner.
